About us

MobilityLab, LLC. was founded in 2011 with one of the leading Russian information technology and system integration organizations, the  I.T. Group of companies (I.T.Co). MobilityLab is an independent software vendor focused on developing the next generation out-of-the-box enterprise mobility solution – MobileSputnik. MobilityLab has been a resident of the SKOLKOVO IT Cluster since 2012. Our leadership team members have average 20+ years of software development, technology, sales and management experience in leading international IT companies including Borland, HP and Microsoft.
